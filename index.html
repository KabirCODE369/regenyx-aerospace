OUR MISSION

Redefining Structural Intelligence.

Regenyx Aerospace is developing adaptive aerospace materials capable of intelligently redistributing structural loads before catastrophic failure.

Through advanced continuum mechanics, biomimetic engineering, and novel load routing concepts, we envision a future where aerospace structures become significantly safer, lighter and longer lasting.

CORE TECHNOLOGIES

Scientific Foundations

CAMLR

Constraint Activated Meta-Stable Load Routing

CAMLR describes adaptive redistribution of structural loads through metastable pathways before critical failure, enabling improved structural resilience.

PTTLR

Phase Transition Triggered Load Routing

PTTLR introduces dynamic load path reconfiguration triggered by localized phase transitions, enabling enhanced damage tolerance.
